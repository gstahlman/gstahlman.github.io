About Me

Education: PhD University of Arizona School of Information, MLS Clarion University, BS New Mexico Institute of Mining & Technology

Research Interests: Exploring the social, institutional, and technical influences that lead to open, replicable, and trustworthy science. I am especially interested in research data management and scholarly communication trends and patterns globally.

Publications: Google Scholar

Projects

Cultivating Equitable and Sustainable Ecosystems for Legacy Research Data
Investigating the value of legacy data for libraries and research communities by exploring existing legacy research data ecosystems and identifying the roles libraries can take in managing these data across disciplines and organizations. Project funded by the Institute of Museum and Library Services (PI with Dr. Inna Kouper, Indiana University)

Advancing the Continued Development and Sustainability of National Public Health Data Dashboards via Mapping and Analysis of the Ecosystem
Advancing theory, research, and practice surrounding design, implementation, and evaluation of public health data dashboards in the U.S. Project funded by the Robert Wood Johnson Foundation (co-PI with Dr. Itzhak Yanovitsky, Rutgers University)

AccelNet-Implementation: The Global Network for Accelerating Synergies Through Research On Astronomy Culture, Communication, Education and Learning (ASTRO ACCEL)
Building an international network of networks to accelerate research, resource development, and resource sharing focused on culture, communications, education/learning, and public engagement in astronomy. Project funded by the National Science Foundation (co-PI with Dr. Tim Spuck, Associated Universities, Inc.)
